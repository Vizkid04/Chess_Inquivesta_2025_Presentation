Rules and Guidelines
General Guidelines
Game play rules


  • This tournament will be conducted according to FIDE rules and regulations.
  • Pairings and standings managed by tournament software.
  • In case of any dispute, chief arbiter's decision is final.
  • Players need to register before the tournament. A form will be provided for the same. Spot entries are strictly forbidden.
  • Online participation certificates will be provided to all players.

  • Players must be seated at their boards at the scheduled time.
  • Late arrival beyond a 10 minute grace period shall result in a default loss.
  • If a player touches a piece (with intent), they must move it if legally possible.
  • If a player touches their opponent's piece (with intent), they must capture it if legally possible.
  • One illegal may be warned. Two illegal moves will result in a loss.
  • Running out of time results in a loss unless the opponent has insufficient mating material.
  • Draw by agreement or threefold repetition or 50 move rule or stalemate or photography.
  • No electronic items such as smart watches, mobile phones, etc, are allowed inside the playing venue. Any violation shall result in an immediate loss.
